Get To Know...

Red Robin

After Dick Grayson takes up the mantle of Batman, Tim Drake (The Third Robin) goes on to tell Spoiler that he will be leaving Gotham for an unspecified period of time. He is now the new Red Robin,an identity that is already tarnished and independent of the Bat-family, traveling the world searching for Bruce Wayne.

Red Hood

Jason Todd, the former Robin killed by the Joker in Batman: A Death in the Family, is revealed to have been resurrected by Ra's al Ghul via the Lazarus Pit. But the pit changes him and his emotions and he becomes the new Red Hood. He actively tries to cleanse the city of corruption, such as the illegal drug trade and gang violence, but in a violent, antiheroic way.

Joker

A failed comedian pressured into committing crime as the Red Hood to support his pregnant wife. Batman's interference causes him to leap into a chemical vat, which disfigures him. This, combined with the trauma of his wife's earlier accidental death, causes him to go insane and become the Joker.
